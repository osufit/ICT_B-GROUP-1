    What is CODECHUM?
    CodeChum is an innovative learning platform for programming education designed to enhance learning and teamwork among students and teachers in a classroom setting. Known as the #1 trusted platform for programming education in the Philippines, CodeChum has partnered with over 100 schools nationwide, providing quality education through their platform.


    Introduction to the Study
    Coding is an essential skill in today’s technology-driven world, supporting everything from apps and AI to banking systems. As demand for coding skills grows, learning to program remains a challenge due to the complexity of translating ideas into precise instructions. Online platforms like CodeChum help simplify programming education by offering interactive lessons, hands-on exercises, and structured courses in languages like C++, Python, and Java. Designed to make coding more accessible, especially in the Philippines, CodeChum provides real-time feedback and a supportive learning environment. However, research on its effectiveness compared to traditional methods remains limited. This study explores how CodeChum impacts ICT students’ programming performance and engagement at Tapinac Senior High School, aiming to enhance programming education.

    Research Questions:
  • What challenges do students face when using CodeChum?
  • What are the benefits of using CodeChum?
  • How does CodeChum influence students' motivation and engagement in programming?
  • What enhancements can be proposed based on the findings?
    •

    Scope and Delimitation
    This study focuses on Grade 11 ICT students at Tapinac Senior High School and examines the perceived impact of CodeChum on their programming skills. It excludes students from other strands, other programming tools, and non-programming-related effects.

    Research Objectives
  • To identify the challenges ICT students face when using CodeChum.
  • To analyze the benefits of CodeChum in programming education.
  • To evaluate CodeChum's effect on students' motivation and engagement.
  • To propose improvements based on research findings to enhance learning.
    •

    Significance of the Study
    The study aims to benefit school administrators, ICT coordinators, teachers, students, and future researchers by providing insights into the effectiveness of CodeChum in programming education. The findings may help in developing better strategies for integrating CodeChum into the learning environment, enhancing teaching methods, and supporting students with diverse learning needs and resources.
